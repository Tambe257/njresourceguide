About The Skin Care Center

The providers and staff at Dr. Ingrid Warmuth Skin Care Center are dedicated to working together as a caring, dynamic team to help manage all of your skin, nail and hair issues through education, diagnosis and individualized treatment. They specialize in high quality dermatologic care in our Elmer and Swedesboro offices and provide up to date information and treatments for various skin conditions, such as acne, warts, psoriasis, rosacea, rashes, precancerous and cancerous lesions and offers comprehensive full body exams, while also providing the latest modern cosmetic medical procedures as well as cyst removals. We are all about educating our patients because we believe that informed patients are better prepared to make decisions about their health and well-being. We look forward to seeing you!

 

Dr. Ingrid P. Warmuth

South Jersey's Leading Dermatologist

 

Dr. Ingrid Warmuth graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of Köln in March 1992, and is the Owner & Medical Director of Warmuth Skin Care Center. She completed her surgical internship at the University Hospital of Köln, Germany. Her residency training was completed in the Department of Dermatology at Columbia University, New York and Presbyterian Medical Center, College of Physicians and Surgeons in New York, New York. Dr. Warmuth was certified by the American Society for Laser Medicine & SurgeryAmerican Board of Dermatology in 1998. Board certification indicates that she has successfully completed additional extensive training and testing in her area of specialty.

 

Dr. Abhishek Aphale

 

Dr. Abhishek Aphale is a board certified Dermatologist with fellowship training in procedural dermatology. He will be completing his fellowship in Philadelphia prior to starting with us full time in July. Dr. Aphale did his residency training at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI and his Doctor of Medicine from Robert Wood Johnson Medical School, New Brunswick, NJ. He specializes in skin cancer detection and treatment as well as Mohs Surgery, excisions and reconstruction. He has extensive training and experience in Cosmetic Dermatology including but not limited to Botox and fillers. Dr. Warmuth and the entire staff welcome Dr. Aphale to the Skin Care Center. Please contact our office today at 856-358-1500 to schedule your next appointment with Dr. Aphale.

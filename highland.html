About Highland Carpet Outlet
 

Bernadette Corwonski

The Corwonski family has owned and operated the business for over 45 years, with two great locations to serve you.

Highland Carpet was originally started by Steve & Andy Corwonski on a 300 acre farm in Malaga NJ. Over the past 45 plus years the Corwonski brothers and their children have run the business. The two brothers have sinced passed and the company is now owned and operated by their children.

Michael is one of the professional installers who installs all types of hard surface flooring. He specializes in vinyl, hardwood and laminate installation.

Bernadette & Tom are the professional sales reps who will take you through the entire process of picking out the flooring, estimating your job, scheduling your appointment to fit your busy life style and arrange for the professional installation.

Paul Garofolo has been with Highland Carpet for over 30 years. He started as an installer and now is one of the professional sales reps who will help you with any carpet or flooring needs.

All of our trained installers have over 30 years of experience in the flooring business.

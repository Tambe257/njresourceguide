Dr. Sayed's Quick Tips

to Stay Healthy During Cold and Flu Season

  • Get your annual flu shot...this is the single best way to prevent seasonal flu
  • Avoid dose contact with people who are sick and stay home from work or school if you are sick
  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water
  • Keep your immune system strong by getting plenty of sleep, eating a healthy diet, drink plenty of water, manage stress and stay physically active
